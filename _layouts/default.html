Welcome to CodeMantra

CodeMantra is a comprehensive learning platform dedicated to providing high- quality educational content in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. The platform offers well-structured learning materials on popular programming languages such as C, C++, Java, and Python, with detailed explanations and practical examples to help learners build strong programming foundations..

In addition to core programming concepts, CodeMantra also delivers insightful content on emerging and advanced technologies including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), Agentic AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Vision Transformers (ViTs).

Our mission is to make complex concepts simple, practical, and accessible for learners at every stage. The platform is designed to support:
  • Undergraduate and postgraduate students
  • Research scholars
  • Academicians and educators
  • Technology enthusiasts and professionals

Whether you are beginning your programming journey or exploring advanced AI- driven technologies, CodeMantra provides the knowledge and resources needed to enhance your technical skills and stay relevant in the rapidly evolving world of technology.

I work on problems related to interpretability, with a focus on large language models and multimodal systems.
